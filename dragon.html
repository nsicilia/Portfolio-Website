Pitches

From our brainstorming, we decided to pitch two of our ideas; the Community Center and the Talent Show.

  • Community Center(including information about community events in the center including educational days, cooking classes, etc.)
  • Talent Show(to highlight the special talents of each participants)

Competitive Analysis

Our pitch for the community center website was approved. From here we each wrote a competitive analysis of other related websites that offer similar information or services. I wrote my competitor analysis based off of the 4th Street Community Center in Calgary, Canada.
